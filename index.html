Respiratory Health

Current air quality may affect people with respiratory conditions.

Moderate risk for asthma patients
Low risk for general population

Recommendations:

  • People with asthma should carry inhalers
  • Limit prolonged outdoor exertion if sensitive
  • Monitor symptoms and seek medical help if needed

Cardiovascular Health

Air pollution can affect heart health, especially in vulnerable populations.

Low risk for most individuals
Moderate risk for elderly with heart conditions

Recommendations:

  • Elderly with heart conditions should avoid heavy exertion
  • Stay hydrated and maintain regular medication
  • Consult doctor if experiencing unusual symptoms

Children & Elderly

Special considerations for vulnerable age groups.

Children should limit prolonged outdoor activities
Elderly should avoid peak pollution hours

Recommendations:

  • Schedule outdoor activities for morning hours
  • Use air purifiers indoors when AQI is elevated
  • Ensure proper ventilation in living spaces
